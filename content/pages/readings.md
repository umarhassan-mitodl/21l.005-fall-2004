Texts
-----

Klaus, Carl H., Miriam Gilbert, and Bradford S. Field, Jr., eds. _Stages of Drama: Classical to Contemporary Theater_. 5th ed. Boston: Bedford/St. Martin’s, 2003. ISBN: 031239733X.
